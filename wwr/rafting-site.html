N'djili River is a top choice for white-water rafting in Kinshasa, and going with a guide is the safest way to tackle Class I to IV rapids. This full-day white-water rafting tour takes you from the Congo Petrol, through Congo-Sep and Kinkole, and down to Maluku. A guide helps you hit the rapids with ease—making this a tour a good choice for kids or beginner rafters—and buffet lunch at Morrisons Rogue Wilderness Lodge is also included to keep you fueled.
