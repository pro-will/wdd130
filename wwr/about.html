White Water Rafting

We aim to deliver thrilling, safe, and unforgettable whitewater rafting adventures that connect people with nature and each other. We believe the river is the perfect place to challenge limits and make lasting memories.

History

Our company has been providing white water rafting trips since 1995. We started with just a few rafts and have grown to become one of the leading rafting companies in the region. It all began with a single raft and a dream. A small crew of friends with a love for the river launched Whitewater Rafting Co. in 2005. From humble beginnings, we've grown into a full-scale adventure outfit trusted by families, thrill-seekers, and corporate groups alike.
